It Program.The word “blogging” means maintaining or
adding content to a blog. People blog for many reasons. Blogs may
provide commentary or news on a particular subject while others
are like personal online diaries. In most cases, a blog includes text, images, and links to other blogs, Web pages, and other
media related to its topic. Most blogs have an interactive format to leave comments. Some blogs may focus on art (art log), photographs (photo blog), sketches (sketch blog), videos (v log), music (MP3 blog), and audio (podcasting). Micro blogging is another type of blogging. It features blogs with very short posts.
